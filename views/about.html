About me

Hello and welcome to my world!

I’m a 23 year old singer, songwriter, make-up artist and fashion blogger. I wanted a place where I could share my music and passions all together so I created this site. You can expect everything from original songs, covers, videos singing live as well as fashion, beauty, fitness and lifestyle posts.

I’m an experienced live singer. I wrote my first original song at the age of 14 and four years later I managed to release it as my first single. In my desire to release more music content I‘ve created my own home studio where I’m recording and mixing all by myself. I perform at events, clubs and weddings. With a diversified and always actualized playlist I can entertain the public with lots of good music in an original way, with passion and professionalism.

Getting myself prepared for concerts made me realize that I enjoy doing my make-up and styling my wardrobe. The latter should come as no surprise because who doesn’t love buying clothes. I will constantly update new looks with refreshing trends and I intend to share my adventures with you.

Dear reader, CI hope to inspire you. I wish you a nice journey and don’t hesitate to share your opinion.
